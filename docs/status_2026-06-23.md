## 🚦 Resume gate — preconditions before re-enabling ANY book

Do not set `MAIN_ENABLED=true` or `PENNY_ENABLED=true` until ALL of these hold.
The first four carry over from status_2026-06-09; the fifth is new.

1. **A signal from a genuinely different class** (not re-mined from the existing
   ~15.5k-report dataset) — post-earnings drift, insider activity, event-driven.
2. **It passes the corrected validator**: next-bar entry, realistic cost, bootstrap
   PF 95% CI entirely above 1.0, consistent across sub-periods.
3. **Money-path bugs fixed** (mostly done 6/09 — verify before trusting live P&L).
4. **Several hundred** independent live paper trades of evidence, not 30–50.
5. **🆕 The bot has its own dedicated Alpaca paper account** — separate from the
   account holding the manual ETF allocation. Otherwise the bot sizes off ~$92k
   instead of its real ~$15.7k sleeve and the dormant 6x sizing bug goes live on
   the first trade. Alpaca paper supports multiple accounts at no cost; drop the
   new account's keys into `user_secrets` for user 1 and fund the bot's intended
   sleeve there.

   Acceptable alternative if you resume before splitting the account: hard-cap the
   bot's sizing to a fixed notional instead of account equity. Splitting is cleaner.

**Trip-wire to watch:** any book flipping to `true` while the ETFs still share the
account. That single change takes the sizing bug from dormant to live.
