## Current trading logic (as deployed)

### Main book (price ≥ $5): `quality_momentum`
A pure factor rule. **BUY when all four hold:** above the 20-day SMA · up on the day · revenue
growth > 0 · profit margin > 0. Otherwise HOLD. The conviction score, bull/bear debate, Claude
review, and edge gates are **bypassed** for main. Long-only; sentiment/Seeking-Alpha is **not**
used here by design (it was part of the no-edge stack).

### Penny book (price < $5): conviction pipeline + gates
The full pipeline runs. **BUY when:** conviction ≥ 7 (rule-based score, adjusted by VIX regime,
1-hour trend, edge-combo history, bull/bear debate, and the Claude analyst) **and** ≥ 2 of 3
edges fire **and** a Sentiment edge is present (the penny gate) **and** catalyst > 0. Penny is
where your Seeking-Alpha flagged emails feed in (sentiment + ticker mentions).

### Exits (both books, checked every 5 min)
Whichever triggers first: hard ATR stop · trailing stop (arms +2%, trails main 3.0% / penny 2.0%
below peak) · take-profit (~1.5R) · partial scale-outs (33% at 1R, 33% at 2R) · 15-day time stop ·
earnings-proximity exit · momentum-divergence stop tighten.

### Risk gates / sizing (both books)
Every BUY must clear: Kelly-calibrated sizing (×0.5 safety, capped 3%/trade; falls back to 1%
risk when historical payoff ≤ 0 — which is the case for main, so new main trades start small) ·
daily-loss limit · 8% drawdown halt · revenge-trading guard · liquidity/ADV · sector cap ·
spread/gap reduction · no entry within 2 days of earnings · duplicate-position block · SPY −2%
circuit breaker. (Risk-of-ruin gate is off by config.)

- `penny_regime.py` — regime-gating experiment (showed penny's edge was one window, not a regime).

- `fetch_hourly_bars` now disk-caches bars (`~/.cache/deepthinktrader/bars/`) → backtests are
